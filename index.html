05 — FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS

How does MindLang work?

+
You send voice messages to our Telegram bot @MindLangSpeachbot. We transcribe your speech using OpenAI Whisper, analyze grammar and pronunciation, suggest corrections, and build a personal vocabulary list. You get instant feedback and a daily study plan based on your mistakes.

Which languages are supported?

+
Currently we fully support English (all levels from A1 to C2). Spanish and Mandarin are coming in Q3 2026. We can transcribe over 50 languages, but grammar corrections and vocabulary features are optimized for English learners at this time.

Is my voice data private?

+
Yes. Voice messages are processed in real-time and deleted immediately after transcription. We do not store audio files. Transcripts are anonymized and used only to improve our AI models. We never sell your data. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

Can I use MindLang for teaching?

+
Absolutely! Our Enterprise plan includes teacher dashboards, student progress tracking, classroom management tools, and bulk discounts for schools and universities. Contact us at enterprise@mindlang.ai for a demo.

What's the difference from ChatGPT?

+
MindLang is specialized for spoken language learning. While ChatGPT is a general chatbot, MindLang focuses on pronunciation, fluency, and natural conversation. We provide detailed grammar feedback, vocabulary tracking, and personalized study plans. Our AI is tuned specifically for ESL learners, not general chat.

How accurate is the transcription?

+
We use OpenAI's Whisper API, which has 98%+ accuracy for clear English speech. Accents are handled well — we've tested with over 50 non-native accents. Background noise may reduce accuracy, so we recommend a quiet environment for best results.

Can I cancel my subscription?

+
Yes, you can cancel anytime from your Telegram bot settings or by contacting support@mindlang.ai. Pro subscriptions are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Annual plans can be refunded within 14 days of purchase.

Do you have an API for developers?

+
Yes! Our REST API allows you to integrate MindLang's voice processing into your own apps. Features include transcription, grammar analysis, and vocabulary extraction. Check our documentation at docs.mindlang.ai for pricing and limits.

How do I get started?

+
Just open Telegram and search for @MindLangSpeachbot. Start the bot and send your first voice message — it's completely free for 50 minutes/month. No signup required, just Telegram.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT) for Pro and Enterprise plans. Enterprise customers can also pay by bank transfer.
